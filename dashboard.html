1 Wildlife & Nature 3 East Circuit: Indri, Green & Nest of Dream

The East of Madagascar is very rich in fauna and flora because it is where Madagascar has its rainforest.

Masoala NP is the biggest rain forest in Madagascar which is home to the endemic wildlife of Madagascar like the Red Ruffed Lemurs, the very rare Helmeted Vanga and the leaf tailed geckos called Uroplatus fimbriatus...
